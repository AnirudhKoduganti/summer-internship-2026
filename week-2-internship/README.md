Accuracy: 
My accuracy on the test paragraphs was 7/10(70%) on the ten test paragraphs from Apple and NVIDIA's 10-K reports. 

The classifier works well when a paragraph contains distinctive keywords that are strongly associated with a particular section. However, it becomes confused when different sections use similar business or financial language. For example, Risk Factors and MD&A both discuss topics such as supply chains, manufacturing, costs, and financial performance, making it difficult for a simple keyword based classifier to distinguish between them. Since the classifier only counts matching keywords and does not understand the context, some paragraphs are misclassified when they contain the same terms.
